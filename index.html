About Our Server

Welcome to New Age Roleplay! We are a dedicated and friendly community focused on providing an immersive and fun roleplaying experience. Our server offers an environment where every interaction matters.

Our staff is committed to maintaining a fair and engaging environment, with regularly scheduled events, strict roleplay rules, and a focus on player enjoyment. Join us and become a part of the best ER:LC community!

What We Offer

An immersive experience

Experience high-quality, realistic roleplay scenarios with a focus on immersive interactions. Our rules promote authentic character development and engaging storytelling.

Daily and Active Events

Participate in exciting server-wide events, from grand pursuits and bank robberies to natural disasters and community gatherings. There's always something happening!

Friendly Community

Join a welcoming and supportive community of ER:LC enthusiasts. Make new friends, form alliances, and create unforgettable memories.

Active & Professional Staff

Our dedicated moderation team is always active, ensuring a smooth experience, fair rule enforcement, and quick resolution of any issues.

Private & Secure

Enjoy a private server environment free from griefers and random players, ensuring a consistent and enjoyable roleplay experience.

Discord Integration

Seamlessly connect with our community through our Discord server for announcements, LFG, general chat, and direct staff support.

How to Join Us

Ready to experience the best ER:LC roleplay? Joining our server is easy!

  1. **Join our Discord Server:** This is mandatory for all members to receive announcements, rules, and connect with the community. Join Discord Now!
  2. **Read the Rules:** Familiarize yourself with our server rules in the Discord server.
  3. **Apply for Whitelist (if applicable):** Some private servers require a brief application. Check our Discord for details.
  4. **Get the Private Server Link:** Once approved or ready, the private server link will be shared in our Discord.
